    Backup and Restore

    Use the dd command to make a full backup of the image:

    dd if=/dev/sdx of=/path/to/image

    or for compression:

    dd if=/dev/sdx | gzip > /path/to/image.gz

    Where sdx is your SD card and the target could be ~/raspbian_wheezy_date "+%Y%m%d_%T". This will save it to your home directory and append the current date and time on the end of the filename.

    To restore the backup you reverse the commands:

    dd if=/path/to/image of=/dev/sdx

    or when compressed:

    gzip -dc /path/to/image.gz | dd of=/dev/sdx
